I am going to write about my three favorite fish. I hope to own these someday!


  1. Oscar Fish
    • Grow to be 10-12 inches
    • Their live span is 10-20 years
    • They are native to the Amazon River



  2. Blood Parrot Fish
    • Grow to be 6-7 inches
    • Their live span is 10-15 years
    • Man-made in Taiwan



  3. Discus Fish
    • Grow to be 5-6 inches
    • Their live span is 10-15 years
    • They are native to the Amazon River just like the Oscar fish
